The materials used for ceremonies differ from one culture to another. We shall mention the common  materials to most cultures in Nigeria. They are:
1. Honey
2. Palm oil
3. Pepper
4. Water
5. Wine
6. Bitter kola
7. Sugar
8. Money
9. Yam
10. Fruits
11. Trophy
12. Ribbons
13. Clothes
14. Soft drings
15. Jewelry
16. Decorated horses
17. Fishing hooks
18. Fishing nets
19. Kola-nuts
20. Masquerade costumes
