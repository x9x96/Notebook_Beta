WAYS OF SHOWING RESPECT

1.	We should greet those around us
2.	When greeting other people, we should be calm and polite. The boys should bow and the girls should curtsy
3.	We should help the elderly people by running errands for them
4.	When elderly people are talking to each other, we should not interrupt them

WAYS OF SHOWING RESPECT TO PLACES AND THINGS

1.	We should keep our community clean
2.	We should keep the school clean
3.	We should be quite and prayerful in places of worship
4.	We should stand at attention when reciting the pledge and singing the national anthem
5.	We must respect time by being punctual always
