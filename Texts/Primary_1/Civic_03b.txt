CAUSES OF ILLNESS

1.	Dirty environment
2.	Mosquito bite
3.	Dirty water
4.	Poisoned food
5.	Picking food from the floor

HOW CAN WE PREVENT ILLNESS

1.	Spray the room and the house with insecticide
2.	Do not expose yourself to cold weather
3.	Avoid eating rotten food
4.	Avoid drinking dirty water
5.	Keep your environment clean
6.	Wash your fruits and vegetables before eating
7.	Wash your hands when you visit the toilet
8.	Eat good food
