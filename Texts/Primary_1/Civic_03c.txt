WHAT TO DO WHEN YOU ARE ILL

1.	We should report to our parents or our teachers.
2.	We should go to the hospital to see a doctor.
3.	We should use medicine recommended by our doctor.
4.	We should also rest well

CARE FOR THOSE WHO ARE ILL

1.	We should visit those who are ill
2.	We should encourage them with kind words
3.	We should help them to do what they cannot do
4.	We should remind them to take their medication
5.	We should assist them see a doctor
6.	We should pray for them
