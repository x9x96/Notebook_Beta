Diskette are flat movable magnetic plates in which information is stored.

Example Of Information Stored In A Diskette Are:
1. School records
2. Records of organizations
3. Games
4. Graphics

Disk Drives: Disk drives are mechanical devices which are used to read or write information from and to a magnetic disk.

Types Of Disk Drives Are:
1. Hard drives
2. Removable drive
3. DVD ROM drives
4. Floppy disk
5. CD ROM drives
