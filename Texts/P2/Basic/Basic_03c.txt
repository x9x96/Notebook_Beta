Some Safety Rules are:
1. Avoid playing with sharp objects like knife and razor blade
2. Avoid fighting or playing roughly
3. Avoid climbing tree or top of roof
4. Do not play near naked wire
5. Be careful when crossing the road. Always look to your left, then right and left again
6. Avoid the use of electric stove without the help of an adult
7. Avoid eating contaminated food or fruits thrown away on the floor
