Air are unseen mixture of gases that surrounds the earth.
Air is useful to us. Air is what we breath in and out.

Air Pollution: Is the release of harmful substances into the air.

Causes Of Air pollution Are:
1. Gas from industries
2. Smoke from car exhaust
3. Odour from the toilet
4. Dust in the air
5. Some from kerosene stove, lamp and firewood

How To Reduce Air Pollution Are:
1. Home should use cooking gas, electric stove instead of firewood
2. Use modern toilet and not pit or bush
3. Industries should be built far away from homes
4. Use electric light and not kerosene lamp or candle

- The air we breath in is called “Oxygen”
- The air we breath out is called “Carbon-dioxide”
