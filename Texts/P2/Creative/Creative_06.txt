Ceremonies are events that make people from far and near come together for a celebration. Local ceremonies are done within a locality

Types Of Ceremonies Are:
1. Local religious ceremonies
2. Local cultural ceremonies

A. Local religious ceremonies: This has to do with man and his maker God. It goes with worship, praise, adoration and supplications. It is spiritual

Examples Of Local Religious Ceremonies Are:
1. Wedding in the church
2. Wedding in the mosques
3. Christmas celebration to mark the birth of Jesus Christ
4. Sallah celebration by the muslims
5. Worship in the shrine
