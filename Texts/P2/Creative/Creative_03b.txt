Types of Stories are:
1.  Real story (Non-Fiction): This is the narration of events that once occurred in a real life time.
2. Imaginative Stories (Fiction): These are the type os stories that are not real. They are only imagined.

How to Create a Good Story
1. Have a story title before you begin your story
2. Have in mind at five moral lessons you want your story to contain
3. Use your imagination to determine different action in the story
4. Assign characters to each action you imagined
5. Be free to confidently use your mind
6. Arrange your story step by stop correctly
7. Write your story in a way everyone will understand
