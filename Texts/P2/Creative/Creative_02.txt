Good morals are those behaviors that are good and acceptable in the society.

Some Good Moral Habits are:
1. Greetings: We should always greet our parents, teachers and elders
2. Honesty: Telling the truth all times
3. Obedience: To our teachers and elders
4. Respect: For parents, teachers and elders
5. Hardworking
6. Good health habit
7. Humility
8. Good eating habits
9. Be kind to people
