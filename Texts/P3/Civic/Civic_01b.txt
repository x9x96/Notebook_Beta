People get infected with HIV through the following ways;

1. Sharing: Sharing of sharp objects such as needle, knife, scissors, clippers with an infected person
2. Blood Transfusion: A person can be infected with HIV through receiving blood that is already contaminated
3. HIV-Infected Mothers: They can also transfer the virus to their unborn children during delivery or breast feeding
4. Through Unprotected S*x: People cannot get infected by hand shaking, hugging, eating with the person or using the same towel with the person
