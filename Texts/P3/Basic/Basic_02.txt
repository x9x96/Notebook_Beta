A colour is the appearance that things have that results from the way in which they reflect light.
It can also mean what an object looks like. Objects are grouped into colours

Groups Of Colours

1. Natural Groups: Examples are sky leaves, flowers, skin, hair, soil, eyes e.t.c
2. Added Colour Groups: Examples are pencil, shoes, clothes, books, tables, bags e.t.c

The chemical used in adding colours to an object are called ‘Dyes’. Dyes have different colours and we can remove dye from these objects
